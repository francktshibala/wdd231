Site Purpose

The site will represent me professionally and showcase my personal projects, skills, and experience as I begin applying for junior developer positions. Using my name ensures clarity, authenticity, and easy recognition.

The Personal Portfolio Website will act as a digital resume and showcase my skills, projects, and experience as a web developer. The site will provide information about:

  • My background and experience.
  • The projects I have worked on with descriptions and links to project repositories.
  • A list of technical skills and tools I am proficient in.
  • A contact form for potential employers or collaborators to reach out to me.

Scenario 1: What projects have you worked on, and where can I see your code?

This will drive the content in the "Projects" section, with detailed descriptions and links to repositories (GitHub links).

Scenario 2: What are your primary skills, and how proficient are you in each?

This will inform the "Skills" section, showcasing my technical abilities and tools I have mastered, such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python...
